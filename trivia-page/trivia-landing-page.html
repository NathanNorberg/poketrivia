Test Your Knowledge!

Gen 1

This generation includes Pokémon from the Red, Blue and Yellow GBC games in which players start their journey across Kanto from their hometown, Pallet Town.

If you select this trivia, then you will be tested on all 151 Pokémon from the Kanto region.

Gen 2

This generation includes Pokémon from the Gold, Silver and Crystal GBC games in which the player begins their journey in the Johto region from New Bark Town.

If you select this trivia, then you will be tested on all 100 Pokémon from the Johto region.
