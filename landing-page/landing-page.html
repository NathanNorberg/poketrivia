About This Project

The creation of this site was inspired by our love for Pokémon. With over 20 years of Pokémon knowledge and experience we feel uniquely qualified to make this. We set out to create a place for pokémon players of all ages to come and test their pokémon knowledge through trivia that spans across all generations. In addition other features such as an all encompassing pokédex and region maps have been added to enahance the users' experience.


We really hope you enjoy what we have built! If you have any feedback, suggestions, or notice any bugs please reach out so we can improve the experience for you and overyone else.
